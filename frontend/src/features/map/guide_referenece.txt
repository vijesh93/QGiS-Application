Here is what goes into those folders as Geoportal grows:

src/features/map/MapView.jsx: This is a Smart Component. Its only job is to talk to the maplibregl library. 
    By separating it, one can swap it for a different map engine later without breaking your sidebar or logic.

hooks/ (The "Logic" folder): In React, "Hooks" are where reusable logic is put. 
    For example, a useMapLayers.js hook would handle the logic of fetching layer data from your FastAPI backend.

components/ (The "UI" folder): These are Dumb Components. 
    They don't know about the map; they just know how to look good. 
    Things like TransparencySlider.jsx or LayerCheckbox.jsx go here.

store/ (The "Brain"): If app expanded for analytics features like calculating averages from layers, etc.
    A "Store" (using zustand) acts like a global variable accessible to every page. 
    It saves the user's selected layers so that if they navigate to an "Analytics" page, 
    the app still remembers what they were looking at.
